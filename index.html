Santiago, Chile

Santiago de Chile is the capital and main urban center of Chile, located in a valley surrounded by the towering Andes Mountains. It’s a vibrant and modern city, blending colonial history with contemporary architecture. Santiago hosts important cultural, financial, and governmental institutions. It’s also known for its parks, like Cerro San Cristóbal, and its proximity to vineyards, ski resorts, and the Pacific coast, making it a versatile and attractive destination.
